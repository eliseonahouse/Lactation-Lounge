Milk Sharing Starts with You.

Some mothers, and many non-traditional families do not have access to breast milk. By buying or selling milk, you can be an integral piece in this community-based project.

We aim to remove accessibility as a factor and enable all babies the opportunity to reap the benefits of milk. Help support the viability of the project by purchasing or selling milk.
